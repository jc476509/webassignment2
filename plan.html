Goals

  1. to boost the sales of tickets for the concerts.
  2. To encorage the performers so that we host their concerts
  3. to also recruit new members for the TMC
  4. To encorage people for volunteering with ease for the TMC

Success Evaluation

  1. To increase the sales of the tickets we have designed in such a way that people will know every thing that is going on within the TCMC i.e, the new events will be displayed in the home page and will be directed to the events page for other events.
  2. To Recruit new members for the TCMC the members application form is made online so that every member can signup online.
  3. We have given the Artists an individual page provided with a list of artists page and also home page which is functional.
  4. We have created the Website in such a way that the people will find it with real ease and also the interface is pretty appealing and good to view.
